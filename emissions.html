Key Insights

  • Emissions peaked in 2007 and have generally decreased since then.
  • The decrease is largely due to shifts from coal to natural gas in electricity generation.
  • Increased use of renewable energy sources has also contributed to the reduction.
  • Despite the overall decrease, emissions are still significantly higher than 1990 levels.
