Climate Change Impacts

Climate change is affecting our planet in numerous ways. Here are some of the key impacts:

Rising Temperatures

Global temperatures have increased by about 1Â°C since pre-industrial times, leading to more frequent heatwaves.

Sea Level Rise

Melting ice sheets and thermal expansion are causing sea levels to rise, threatening coastal communities.

Extreme Weather Events

Climate change increases the frequency and intensity of hurricanes, droughts, and floods.

Biodiversity Loss

Changing climates are forcing species to adapt or migrate, leading to ecosystem disruptions.

Long-term Consequences

  • Food security challenges due to changing agricultural conditions.
  • Water scarcity in some regions due to altered precipitation patterns.
  • Increased health risks from heat-related illnesses.
  • Economic impacts from damage to infrastructure.
